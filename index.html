About Me

After graduating college with a Master's of Arts in Teaching, I became an elementary school teacher. I have taught in the classroom for eight years. While I love teaching, I also really enjoy learning myself. Because my daily lessons hinged on technology such as websites and applications, I became curious about their development.

Outside of web development, I relish in listening to podcasts, reading books, watching inspiring films and shows, and being outdoors. My family and I appreciate nature and travel to many National Parks.

As an aspiring student at Thinkful, I've just begun my journey to becoming a software developer and with a lot of determination, perseverance, and grit, I will succeed!
